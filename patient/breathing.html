Box Breathing

A powerful technique used to relieve stress and improve concentration by creating a steady, even breathing rhythm.

Best for: Focus, calm, and resetting after stress.

The Pattern
  • Inhale through nose (4s)
  • Hold breath (4s)
  • Exhale through mouth (4s)
  • Hold empty breath (4s)
